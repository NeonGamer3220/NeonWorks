Questions

FAQ

A few quick answers. If something isn’t covered here, just reach out and ask.

What kind of websites do you build?

Mostly small, focused sites: one‑pagers, portfolios, simple business sites and tiny web apps. If your idea is huge or very complex, we can still talk and maybe carve out a smaller first version.

How long does a project usually take?

For a small site, expect roughly 2–3 weeks from “let’s do it” to launch, depending on how quickly we lock in copy and feedback. Bigger or more custom things can take a bit longer.

Do you use templates or page builders?

The layouts at NeonWorks are hand‑built with HTML, CSS and a bit of JavaScript. That keeps the sites light and makes it easier to tweak things exactly to your project.

Can you help with hosting and domain setup?

Yes – I can help you point your domain, choose a simple hosting option and get everything online. The idea is that by the end you have a link you can share without worrying about the tech.
